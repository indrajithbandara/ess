Checking In and Out

{% include svg/use.html id="icn--clock" class="clock" %} Check In: 14.00 {% include svg/use.html id="icn--clock" class="clock" %} Check Out: 12.00

Want to continue using the room after checkout time because of an evening - or night flight? This might be possible depending on occupancy. Please consult our management. It will be free of charge until 16:00.

After 16:00, the full room rate will be charged. In all cases your luggage can be safely stored and all facilities can be freely used.

{% include svg/use.html id="icn--clock" class="clock" %} Skateboarding: 9:00am - 9:00pm

Deposit and Payment Policy

We require deposit of 50% at the time of booking if the booking is earlier than 31 days before check in. The balance is due 31 days before check in. Full payment is required if the booking is made less than 30 days before check in. We accept Visa or Mastercard.

Room rates are subject to change without prior notification, confirmed reservations will not be affected.

Cancellation Policy

50% will be charged if the reservation is cancelled 30 days prior arrival. If the reservation is cancelled closer to the arrival date no refund.
