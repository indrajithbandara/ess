Checking In and Out

Check In: 14.00 Check Out: 12.00

Want to continue using the room after checkout time because of an evening - or night flight? This might be possible depending on occupancy. Please consult our management. It will be free of charge until 16:00.

After 16:00, the full room rate will be charged. In all cases your luggage can be safely stored and all facilities can be freely used.

Breakfast

Breakfast: 7.30

Our guests can order breakfast starting at 7.30am. It can be delivered to the room.

Skateboarding

Skateboarding: 9:00am - 9:00pm

Our guests can use the bowl everyday with no need to pay the skateboarding fee. If you want your kid to have private skateboarding lessons you can! We offer a skate school for beginners and intermediate with experienced instructors.

Deposit and Payment Policy

We do not require a deposit, only a booking guarantee. The credit card details, you are asked to enter during the booking procedure, are used for the booking guarantee. However nothing will charged to your account at the booking stage. Full payment will happen at check-out at the resort. We accept Visa, MasterCard or cash payment.

Room rates are subject to change without prior notification, confirmed reservations will not be affected.

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation must be received within 14 days, or more, prior to arrival (depending on the rate plan booked). If the reservation is cancelled closer to the arrival date, 100% of the first night will be charged (depending on the rate plan booked). In case of a no-show, a cancellation fee, equal to the number of nights cancelled, will be charged.
